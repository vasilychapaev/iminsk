Shopping centers

    A wide selection of clothes, footwear, cosmetics and household goods.

    Working hours: open from M-Sa 10am-10pm

  • GUM

    Nezavisimost Ave-21, subway station "Kastrychnitskaya"

  • TSUM

    Nezavisimost Ave-54, subway station "Ploscha Yacuba Kolasa"
